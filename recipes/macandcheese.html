Awesome Mac and Cheese Recipe

This is my son's favorite Mac and Cheese recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Marconi pasta
  • Water
  • Cheese powder stuff
  • Milk to make it taste better.

Steps:

  1. Boil water.
  2. Add pasta to boiling water.
  3. Boil for 5 - 8 minutes.
  4. Strain pasta.
  5. Add cheese powder stuff and milk.

Warning: This recipe includes dairy products.
