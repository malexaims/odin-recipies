Awesome Ham Sandwich Recipe

This is my son's favorite Ham Sandwich recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Ham Slices
  • American cheese
  • Mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Put one slice of cheese onto the bread, or multiple slices if you really like cheese.
  3. Apply up to three slices of ham, because it is a ham sandwich after all!
  4. Add Mayonnaise
  5. Assemble the sandwich
